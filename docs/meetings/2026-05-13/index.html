Regular — May 13, 2026

1h 56m · YouTube · Simbli

The Board was scheduled to consider multiple infrastructure and sustainability initiatives, including approval of agreements for 8 EV chargers at Hoover Community School and Roy Cloud School through PowerFlex, plus installation agreements with Atlas Pellizzari Electric ($70,520) for chargers at Adelante Selby, Clifford, and Kennedy Schools. The agenda also proposed discussions of school plans for North Star School and Henry Ford School, migration of district servers to Microsoft Azure Cloud Services via Looking Point, and approval of numerous board policies related to student conduct, health benefits, and academic programs.
