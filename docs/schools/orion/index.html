Redwood City School District

Orion Alternative School

Orion is a TK-5 alternative school of choice offering Mandarin dual-language immersion with a parent participation (co-op) model. It is the most ethnically diverse school in the district. Achievement gaps exist within the school between White and Hispanic students.

School of Choice iFamilies from anywhere in the district can apply to attend a School of Choice through the open enrollment process.

Growth measures how much students are learning each year, regardless of their starting point. A school with low proficiency but high growth is accelerating learning. Proficiency measures how many students are at or above grade level right now.

Growth = % of students achieving 105%+ of expected growth on the CAASPP (California Assessment of Student Performance and Progress), the annual state standardized test for grades 3–8. Proficiency = % meeting or exceeding grade-level standards.

Sources: 2024-25 SARCs, 2025-26 SPSAs, 2025-26 LCAP, HR data briefing (Feb 2026). Proficiency from 2023-24 CA state testing. Student growth from 2024-25 evaluations.
