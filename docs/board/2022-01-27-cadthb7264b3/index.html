Meeting Minutes Special Board Meeting - Board Docs Training (Thursday, January 27, 2022) Approved Generated by Rebecca Alvarez on Friday, January 28, 2022 1. Call to Order - Public Session The meeting was called to order by President Kepner at 5:00 PM Roll Call Members present: Susan Fish, Randi Kinman, Robert Owens, Jack Lucas, Anne Kepner Student Trustees: Gabrielle Wilson via zoom Not present: Karl Watanabe and Adrienne Grey 3.3 Pledge of Allegiance The pledge of allegiance was led by Trustee Lucas. Discussion: Board Docs Training Board Docs Trainer Diana Roadcap led a Board Docs training for the Board of Trustees. The district is transitioning to utilizing Board Docs in an effort to streamline processes. A few highlights from the training included: Every organization has its own unique URL. Logging in, viewing the agenda and the attachments. Navigation to agendas, meeting minutes, and the library feature. Access to content, private vs. public. There is no limit on the number of meetings, agendas, and other documents that can be stored on Board Docs. The Board Docs help feature. Questions were asked and addressed during the training. 2. Adjournment The meeting was adjourned at 6:00 PM
