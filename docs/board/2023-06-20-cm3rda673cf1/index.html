Board Member Virtual Participation pursuant to AB 2449 Remote Teleconference Attendance for &quot;Just Cause&quot; or with a qualifying &quot;Emergency Circumstance&quot;

1. Assembly Bill 2449 Remote Teleconference Attendance Pursuant to 2449, a trustee may be eligible to attend a Board of Education meeting remotely with &ldquo;Just Cause&rdquo; or with a qualifying &ldquo;Emergency Circumstance.&rdquo; To ensure compliance with the notice requirements of AB 2449, the Board will maintain an AB 2449 standing agenda item for each meeting. Identify Just Cause submissions, if any . Pursuant to 2449, a trustee may attend the Board meeting remotely with a qualifying Just Cause. Just Cause includes a childcare or caregiving need, a contagious illness that prevents a trustee from attending in person, a need related to a qualifying physical or mental disability, and travel while on official business of the Board or another state or local agency.
