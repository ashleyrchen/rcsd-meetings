Considered draft until approved during the February 15, 2022 Board of Trustees Meeting Meeting Minutes Special Joint Meeting with the Saratoga City Council, 6:00 PM (Wednesday, January 19, 2022) Generated by Rebecca Alvarez on Thursday, January 20, 2022 Joint Session with WVMCCD Board and Saratoga City Council Discussion, Information: 1.1 Joint Session with WVMCCD and the Saratoga City Council The meeting was called to order at 6:02 PM The following members were present: City of Saratoga Council Members Tina Walia, Mayor, Kookie Fitzsimmons, Vice Mayor, Mary-Lynne Bernald, Council Member, Rishi Kumar, Council Member, Yan Zhao, Council Member, James Lindsay, City Manager, Crystal Bothelio, Assistant City Manager West Valley-Mission Community College District Members Bradley Davis, Adrienne Grey, Randi Kinman, Susan Fish, Jack Lucas, Bob Owens, Stephanie Kashima, Manny Cappello Discussion Topics Introductions The Saratoga City Council introduced themselves, then the West-Valley Mission Community College Members did the same. Updates Stephanie Kashima provided an update on current West Valley College highlights. Chancellor Davis thanked President Kashima for continuing the good partnership between City of Saratoga and West Valley College. He went on to provide an update. Discussion ensued. No public comment was made. Other Remarks & Wrap Up The Council thanked the Board of Trustees, Chancellor Davis and President Kashima for their continued partnership and their time this evening. The meeting was adjourned at 6:26 PM
