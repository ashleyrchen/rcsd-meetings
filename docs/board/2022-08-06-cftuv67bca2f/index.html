WVMCCD Board of Trustees Special Meeting - Mandated Training (Saturday, August 6, 2022) - unapproved Generated by Rebecca Alvarez on Wednesday, August 17, 2022 Public Session Meeting 1. Meeting Opening The meeting was called to order at 10:05 AM 1.1 Roll Call Members present Susan Fish, Robert Owens, Karl Watanabe, Jack Lucas, Anne Kepner, and Adrienne Grey Absent: Randi Kinman 1.2 Pledge of Allegiance The pledge of allegiance was recited. 1.3 Brown Act Training - Governing Board Teleconference Meetings in the 21st Century - Presented by: Kathryn Meola, Partner with AALRR Kathryn Meola provided a training on Governing Board Teleconference Meetings in the 21st Century. The Agenda included: Intent of the Brown Act General Rules and Terms Defined Brown Act rules for social media Notice of Meetings & Public Participation Closed Session Teleconference Meetings under AB 361 and under the Traditional Rules (pre and post pandemic) There were questions from the Board Members which were addressed. 1.4 Ethics & Conflicts of Interest for Board Members - Presented by: Kathryn Meola, Partner with AALRR Kathryn Meola provided a training on Ethics & Conflicts of Interest. The agenda included: Ethical Behavior and Duties of Public Officials Conflicts of Interest - The Law Gov't Code 1090 (Contracts) Political Reform Act (Use of Official Position) Common Law Conflicts of Interest (Divided Loyalties) Economic Interest Disclosure: FPPC Form 700 Other Considerations There were questions from the Board Members which were addressed. 2. Adjournment The special training meeting was adjourned at 12:00 PM
