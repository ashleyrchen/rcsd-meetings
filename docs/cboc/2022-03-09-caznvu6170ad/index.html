MEASURE C & MEASURE W BOND OVERSIGHT COMMITTEE Approved Minutes March 9. 2022 1.0 Meeting opening 1.1 Call to Order The meeting was called to order at 5:33pm by Mike Foulkes, committee vice chair. 1.2 Roll Call X Shirley Cantu Taxpayer Organization X Paul Cavagnolo Community-at-Large X Karen DiLullo Business Organization Brigit Espinosa &ndash; Chair Community-at-Large X Mike Foulkes &ndash; Vice Chair District/College Support Organization X Bill Hult Senior Organization X Travis Ginn WVC Student Organization 1.3 Public Comments (three minute limit per person) None. 2.0 Regular Order of the Agenda 2.1 Approval of the order of the agenda The order of the agenda was approved as presented. (6/0/0, with Espinosa absent and not voting) 2.2 Approval of the December 8, 2021, meeting minutes The December 8, 2021, minutes were approved as presented. (6/0/0, with Espinosa absent and not voting) 3.0 ACTION ITEMS 3.1 Review and approval of the 2021 Annual Report (A) Committee members reviewed the annual report. Ms. Cantu, who was the committee chair during the reporting period, thanked members for their commitment and being able to consistently achieve a quorum. There was a motion by Hult, seconded by Cavagnolo, to &ldquo;approve the 2021 Annual Report as presented.&rdquo; The motion passed 6/0/0 with Espinosa absent and not voting. Ms. Booth reported that the Annual Report would be presented to the Board at its April 19 th meeting. 4.0 REPORTS/UPDATES 4.1 Chancellor&rsquo;s Report Chancellor Davis reported that student enrollment has continued to decline statewide and that the District is offering an early retirement program in an attempt to downsize faculty/staff to better match the current enrollment. The chancellor also reported that the District is conducting a feasibility study regarding housing on the campuses. 4.2 Construction and Financial Update Ms. Reyes-Bolinger presented the Project Progress Report for Mission College. Ms. Dabalos presented the Project Progress Report for West Valley College. Mr. Hult again thanked staff for the excellent, well-developed information and the beautiful visual presentation. Mr. Castruita reviewed the program schedule for Mission College. He then reported that the Measure W Project Priority List was revised in December. The following revisions were made: District Services: DS-10 Administration Building. Changes were made to this project to support the Mission College Child Development Center (CDC) Renovation project. Funding from this project will be used to supplement the construction bid award of the CDC Renovation project as follows: DS-10 Administrative Building funding: ($2,000,000) Mission College: MC-03 CDC Building Renovation. The budget for this project will be increased by $3,250,000 to cover construction bid award, a change order contingency, furniture and equipment, and an audio/video system. Funding for this increase is derived from Measure W funds as follows: MC-01 Program Contingency funding: $1,250,000 DS-10 Administrative Building funding: $2,000,000 Mr. Castruita and Ms. Ryan reviewed the Measure C and Measure W financials. MC-05 &ndash; Library Renovation at Mission College was highlighted during the review of the Measure W financials. Measure C is approximately 89% complete and the second series of bonds for Measure W will be issued in July to ensure that construction can continue on schedule. Mr. Castruita noted that construction has slowed due to supply chain issues related to the pandemic. Ms. Cantu inquired about contingency funds being nearly exhausted. Mr. Castruita responded that contingency funds for Measure W have been used more quickly due to cost escalation issues. 4.3 Committee member term status Ms. Booth reported that the membership term for three members would expire in April and asked if they would agree to serve another term consistent with the committee by-laws. The three members, Karen DiLullo, Travis Ginn, and Paul Cavagnolo agreed to the new term. The committee member names will be presented to the Board for re-appointment at the April 19 th meeting. 5.0 CLOSING ITEMS 5.1 Staff Member Comments Mr. Castruita, Ms. Ryan, and Ms. Dabalos reported that they would be retiring on or prior to June 30, 2022. All three will be missed. Chancellor Davis indicated that the District is already planning for the transition. 5.2 Committee Member Comments Mr. Foulkes indicated that he has served on many committees and that the District&rsquo;s team in one of the best he has seen. 5.3 Adjournment Meeting adjourned at 7:05pm.
